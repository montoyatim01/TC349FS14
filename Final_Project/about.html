About

Currenty a Media and Information major at Michigan State University, Timothy started out exploring his passion in photography. As he moved through high school and in to college, he found that his passion moved from still photos, to moving photos. Going into film for a career was an obvious choice.


Since starting his career at MSU, Tim has been involved in many out of school activities. He joined the MSU Filmmakers club, taking the title of president for his Junior year. He has been the videographer for the MSU Formula Racing Team for three years. He has also worked on several side projects.
